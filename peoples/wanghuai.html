Wanghuai received B.Sc degree majored in Materials Science & Engineering from Taiyuan University of Technology at 2007. After that he moved to the Institute of Solid State Physics, Chinese Academy of Sciences, to pursue his PhD on condensed matter physics. After he got his PhD degree in 2014, he became a lecturer at the Department of Material Physics, Hubei University of Automotive Technology. In 2018, he got a scholarship from the China Scholarship Council and became a visiting postdoc fellow at the Huo’s group.Wanghuai’s research interests mainly focus on developing new non-adiabatic method. He has developed a code based on SHARC to implement the Quasi-Diabatic method developed by Huo group, which can in principle combined any diabatic dynamics method with adiabatic quantum chemistry calculation. Another interesting topic is non-adiabatic dynamic simulation of molecules in cavity. He is now developing some mean-field method to simulate the dynamics of molecules in cavity.
