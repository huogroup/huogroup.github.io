Prakhar Swarup received dual degree in BS MS (Chemistry major and Physics minor) from Indian Institute of Science Education and Research Thiruvananthapuram (IISER-TVM), India in May, 2018. During his BS MS, he worked on three research projects: MS project (2018) at IISER-TVM, India; Summer project (2017) at UNIST, S. Korea; Summer project (2016) at S. N. Bose National Centre for Basic Sciences (SNBNCBS), India. Subsequently, he joined graduate program at University of Rochester, USA in August 2018. In, December, 2018 he joined Prof. Pengfei Huo’s group.
