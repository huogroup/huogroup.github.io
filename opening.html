Openings

Post-Doc

If you are interested in joining the group, please send a resume and a brief letter of introduction to huo@chem.rochester.edu.

Graduate Students

The Huo group is currently accepting new graduate students. If you are interested in joining the group, please contact Professor Huo. Prospective graduate students please apply to the Graduate Program in Chemistry at University of Rochester.

Undergrad Students

Our group always welcome undergraduate students who are interested in Theoretical and Computational Chemistry.
For undergraduate students at the University of Rochester, please check the Discover Grants and the undergraduate research opportunities for more information.
For domestic students, please check Research Experience for Undergraduates (REU) for more information.
For International students, please check the Summer Research Fellowships for International Students for more information.
